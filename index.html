Judy Hopps is a bunny and the main character in Disney 2016 film Zootopia. She is a rabbit living in BunnyBurrow on her family carrot farm. She dreams of being a police officer, in the big city of Zootopia, even though there never been a bunny cop before. Alot of others animals try to crush her dreams, but Judy is fiercly determinated. I think Judy is a wonderful example of how anyone human or animal can reach there dreams if they never give up.

The story continues with Judy joining the police force, but initially not being accepted by her boss or fellow officers. when Mrs. Otterton comes to the station, destraught over the dispearance of her husband Emmmit Otterton. Judy offers to take the case, staking her career on it. Following her only lead Judy hustles a fox named Nick into helping her and the two form an unexpected friendship while working together. There are some set backs along the way, but in the end she imerges victorious while learning alot about herself and the reality of life.
